Accessibility Services
Physical Accessibility
  • Wheelchair accessible entrances and elevators
  • Accessible restrooms on all floors
  • Wheelchair rentals available free of charge
  • Designated accessible parking spaces
Visual Accessibility
  • Large-print exhibition guides
  • Braille exhibition labels and maps
  • Descriptive audio tours
  • Tactile tours available by appointment
Hearing Accessibility
  • Assistive listening devices for guided tours
  • Sign language interpretation (with 48-hour notice)
  • Captioned video content
Sensory Accessibility
  • Sensory-friendly hours: First Tuesday of each month, 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
  • Quiet rooms available
  • Sensory maps indicating sound and light levels

For specific accessibility requests or to arrange accommodations, please contact our Visitor Services team at access@artegallery.com or call +92 (123) 456-7890 at least 48 hours in advance of your visit.
